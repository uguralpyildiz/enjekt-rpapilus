Building Digital Brands Since 2020

In 2020, Nowify Media’s founders saw the power of digital strategy to connect brands with people. They built the company around a human-centered approach, blending creativity and data to drive meaningful growth.

Today, Nowify Media is a trusted partner for global brands, delivering impactful marketing solutions with a passionate and skilled team.

Şahsenem Saphira | Co-Founder

Şahsenem Saphira is the Co-Founder and CEO of Nowify Media, where digital strategy meets a human-centered approach. Raised in Germany in a multicultural family, she is fluent in German, English, Turkish, and Spanishnavigating effortlessly between ideas, cultures, and disciplines.

With a background in psychology, she blends structure with empathy to help brands and individuals turn their visions into focused, meaningful action. Today, her clients span the globe, including the United States, Europe, Asia, and the Arab world.

She believes that business shouldn’t just functionit should resonate.

“If the world doesn’t know you, you simply haven’t been told right yet.”
– Şahsenem Saphira
