TOP 5 Design Inspiration Sites to Boost Your Creativity
TOP 5 Design Inspiration Sites to Boost Your Creativity
Documentation
4 min
Published on Dec 08, 2025

Use AI to summarize this article

Key points

In this article:

  • Provides a curated list of the top 5 design inspiration websites to help boost creativity and spark new ideas for visual projects.
  • Highlights platforms that cater to various design needs — from portfolios and community showcases to landing page and color-based inspiration.
  • Explains how each resource offers unique value, whether you’re seeking trend insights, curated galleries, or dynamic creative examples.
Who is this article for?

This article is intended for designers, creatives, UX/UI professionals, and anyone involved in visual or web design who needs reliable sources of inspiration to support their creative process and elevate project outcomes.

Creativity is often fueled by inspiration. For all designers, whether beginners or experts, the quest for new ideas can require long hours spent browsing various websites. In this article, we compile a list of the 10 best sites to find design inspiration.
1. Behance: Adobe's Creative Hub
Behance is much more than just an inspiration site; it’s a multifunctional platform. Owned by Adobe, Behance is a social network that unites a large community of designers across all disciplines.

Here, you will find inspirations ranging from 3D to photography, web design to innovative products.

You can also find job listings and even purchase graphic elements directly from designers.

A must for all freelance creatives, Behance can serve as your portfolio where you can showcase your various projects.
2. Dribbble: The Gathering Place for Web Designers
Similar to Behance, Dribbble is a favorite spot for designers worldwide. On this social network, you’ll find a variety of illustrations, 3D designs, landing page inspirations, and other web design elements. As a bonus, they also offer an online training program for aspiring web designers.
3. Awwwards: The Podium for Best Designs
Awwwards is not just an inspiration site; it’s a competition that rewards the best websites in the world. Whether you are looking for stunning animations or innovative designs, Awwwards is the perfect place to discover current trends.
4. Designspiration: A Dive into Colors
This site goes well beyond simple web design inspirations. Designspiration allows you to explore various graphic universes based on one or more colors. Specifically, you select the colors that interest you and Designspiration automatically filters inspirations that match. Ideal for creating mood boards or finding a unique color palette.
5. Brutalistwebsites: For Lovers of Brutalism
If you are fascinated by raw and minimalist design, this site is a goldmine. Simple yet powerful, it gathers the best brutalist designs from the web.
